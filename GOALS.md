# Theme — Goals

## What we're building
A high-converting Shopify storefront for ogresells.com that replicates the lukesvendors.com design and feel — but for Oscar's brand and products.

## Short-term (current sprint)
Fix what's broken, then build what's missing:

1. **Fix buttons** — audit and standardize across all sections
2. **Fix sticky header** — correct scroll behavior, stays above urgency bar
3. **Fix cart** — working cart drawer or redirect
4. **Fix footer** — final polish
5. **Build header section** — logo, nav, CTA button, full theme settings
6. **Build testimonials** — screenshot carousel, infinite scroll, hover pause
7. **Build FAQ** — accordion, first open by default, accent glow on open
8. **Build reviews** — card grid, write-a-review modal

## Medium-term (v2 launch)
- All sections complete and matching lukesvendors.com quality
- Mobile experience polished (slightly higher priority than desktop)
- Theme settings cover every visual element — store owner can configure everything
- Push v2 to Shopify and replace V1 live

## Long-term
- Apply learnings to Vexel (the theme product sold to other resellers)
- Build new sections Oscar needs as the business grows

## What success looks like
- Page loads fast (no lag on initial visit)
- Looks and feels like lukesvendors.com — same energy, adapted for OGResell brand
- Every section configurable through Shopify theme editor
- Mobile looks as good as desktop
